Meme Night: Free for ages 17 and under and $10 admission for 18+.

11/5/2018 is our 49th bi-weekly meme night. Come down for an entire catalog of meme-based drinks and food for free as we watch viral videos. You could even submit your own meme if you cannot find your favorite in our catalog. Pending approval, of course. The best user-submitted meme of the night would be given a custom-made stencil and a free personal sized dessert to take home.

Young Artist Recognition Contest: For ages 29 and under. Opens 11/2/2018. Deadline 11/29/2018.

Despite running a business and raising a child, many times at once, Marosa Giorgio never lost sight of her love of art. To honor her memory, we like to hold an annual contest to showcase small artists. The 5th annual YARC is divided into 2 categories: stencils and edible prints. Stencils need to not be overly complex, but need to be attention grabbing and minimalistic. Edible prints can be more complex, however, you need to be weary of colors, as the color range that excite people and doesn't ruin their appetite is much more limited. All submissions must be not be based on existing intellectual properties. 1st place winners will have their submission as a permanent part of our menu and be given a $500 cash prize. 2nd and 3rd place winners will get $150 and $50 respectively. Winners are decided based on popular vote in store and online. A special honorable mention will be chosen by the staff and get a $50 prize.


Anime Night: Age depends on content shown on particular night. Fridays and Saturdays.

11/2/2018 marks another anime night for the Edible Art Cafe. This Friday we will marathon Jojo's Bizarre Adventure, concluding the night with the premiere of the latest episode of Part 5 (Vento Aureo). Get drinks and food based on anime characters of the past and present. Submit your own anime image of you can't find your favorite character in our catalog. The best user-submitted image of the night will be given a custom-made stencil and a free personal sized dessert to take home. Given the content of the show, this night is restricted to 17+.
11/3/2018 will be a Studio Ghibli marathon.
